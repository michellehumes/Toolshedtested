## Buying Guide

### Blade Length
- **18-20 inches:** Ideal for small hedges and detailed shaping
- **22-24 inches:** Best all-around for most homeowners
- **26+ inches:** Faster for long, straight hedges

### Battery Compatibility
Check if the trimmer uses batteries from tools you already own. Most major brands (DeWalt, Milwaukee, Makita, Ryobi) keep batteries compatible across their lineups.
