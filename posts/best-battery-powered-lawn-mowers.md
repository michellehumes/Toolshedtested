## FAQ

**Q: How long do battery mower batteries last?**
A: Quality lithium-ion batteries last 5-8 years or 500+ charge cycles.

**Q: Are battery mowers quieter than gas?**
A: Yes, significantly. Most operate at 65-75 dB vs. 85-95 for gas.
